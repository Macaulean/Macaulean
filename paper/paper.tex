\begin{abstract}

  Our project aims to build tools that ease the use of Macaulay2 and potentially other computer algebra systems with Lean.
Our current work focuses on using Macaualy2 to prove statements over commutative rings by using Macaulay2 to construct certificates. This includes in particular work with ideal membership and Groebner bases.
This extend abstract discuss our approach and strategy along with current progress and future goals.
